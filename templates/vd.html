The Way to get wealth; or, An easie way to make wine ... A help to discourse, and divers curiosities - Lupton, Thomas, 1702

This artifact shows the origin of the production of sugar in Venice - a book written in 1702 attributes the refinement of sugar to a Venetian 200 years ago. This helps clarify how sugar began to be further developed and fleshed out as a trade in Italy (especially Venice) around the Renaissance.
