The Pseudo Scientific Racialization of Slavery

Pseudoscientific justifications for racism have been around for centuries. Once the sugar industry became a main source of income for many Europeans, they needed a way of producing sugar more efficiently. The Europeans used slavery to maximize the production of sugar. In order to motivate the slaves to work, they threatened and severely hurt them. To get away with these inhumane actions under their religion and laws, they needed to justify their actions. They used pseudoscientific documents to justify the bad behavior. They used pseudoscientific documents talking about brain and skull size, pain tolerance, etc.
